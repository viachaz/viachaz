Software Behind My Business

The companies listed in this article do not sponsor me directly. However, the companies that offer referral links, I used them to aide with my freelance journey.

Notion

You can use Notion to you can run the backend of your business as an entrepreneur or small business. Notion features notes, docs, spreadsheets, databases, knowledge bases, tasks, projects, and much more. Check out their templates, to see how you can optimize your business

Webflow

I create most of my websites with Webflow. Webflow allows me to build customized websites that perform like champs since you don't need 100's of plug-ins. It's simple to use and offer a content management system for straightforward updates.

Calendy

Calendy is a scheduling tool that integrates with your calendar of choice. The software checks your availability and helps you connect with your contacts, prospects, and clients. I use it for consultations, on-boarding, and general meetings. There are options to send a link and to embed directly into your website. Schedule a free consultation with me to discuss your business outlook.

ConvertKit

ConvertKit is an email marketing and campaign tool specialized for creators. With ConvertKit, it’s easy to customize and embed forms on your website to turn casual readers into subscribers. Don’t have a website? You can build a landing page to start growing your list today. Email marketing is far from dead.

Bonsai

Bonsai is a software used to send out proposals, contracts, and invoices. Making it easy for people to pay you (while ensuring you can track it all) is a critical thing for budding entrepreneurs.

Rebrandly

Rebrandly is a link shortener great for tracking, so instead of the link to this article being via.com/blog/2019/Software-Behind-The-Business I can make it viachaz.space/favoite-tools. I prefer Rebrandly over other URL shorteners because you can customize the URL to keep everything on-brand.

Canva

Canva is best for DIY designs. To thrive as an entrepreneur, you have to cut out the middleman sometimes. Create a simple social media cover, logo, and much more with Canva.

Unsplash

You won't find a better place for aggregated, high-resolution, license-free images.

Salesmate

Salesmate is an awesome pipelining tool and CRM great for moving people or things through a funnel. I.e. Contact, Pitched, Proposal Sent, Proposal Accepted.

1password

1password remembers all of your passwords for you. Save your passwords and log in to sites with a single click. It's that simple.

Of course, where it makes sense, have a professional help you out with these. If you need a custom plan for what to use for your specific industry, I'm happy to help. It's a sit-down session or a video chat that will save you time.
